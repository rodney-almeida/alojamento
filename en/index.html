About Us

We are a small family looking to establish roots in our beloved Portugal, after living abroad for a few years.

A little bit of our history:
Rodney was born in Australia, where his parents moved to in late 70s. Silvia was born and raised in Portugal, near Lisbon. Rodney was always passionate about Portugal until one day he decided to come and try it out. That when our joint story began. We decided to move to Australia for Silvia to have a bit of a taste of the country down under. There we had some of the best adventures of our lives.

We love to camp, bushwalk, hike and go for a dip in the river and we always imagined our lives to be spent close to nature. In this beautiful little village, surrounded by all the things we love we found our little piece of paradise and would love to share it with anyone who wants to spend some time in nature.
